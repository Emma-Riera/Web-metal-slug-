Metal Slug

Metal Slug

Metal Slug is a shooter game in which you, playing as Marco Rosso, join forces with the government in order to destroy the rebellion. This is done through a hallway shooter, where you can only move in one direction and, armed with your gun, must defeat all enemies in front of you.

Explota Studios

Members: Emma, Aidan, Bernat and Pau.
